About Us

暁 - あかつき

Japanese fresh food restaurant begins its story with the ultimate desire to bring the quintessence of Japanese cuisine to Vietnamese diners. That aspiration is rekindled from the endless passion, together with the determination to realize the big dream in our life. All is the pursuit of passion, dedicated service and understanding of the taste of diners.

THE BEST OF VINH CITY

Consistently recognized as one of the best Japanese restaurants in Vinh City, Akatsuki Restaurant & Sushi Bar offers everything you’re looking for in authentic Japanese-style food. From the freshest sushi and sashimi to a variety of flavorful delicacies, we pride ourselves on quality and exceptional service. Additionally, enjoy live entertainment with a DJ and dancing every Friday and Saturday night. Come and experience for yourself why Akatsuki is the top choice in Vinh City.

Enjoy Our Food

Akatsuki Japanese Restaurant & Sushi Bar offers innovative sushi and Japanese cuisine. When you dine with us, you can expect a truly stimulating experience.

Discover Our Menu

Akatsuki restaurant is renowned not only for its sashimi from the fresh source of ingredients (either fished domestically or directly delivered from famous Tsukiji and Osaka seafood market in Japan) but also for Yakitori & Beer Kitchen corner – The provenance of grilled Wagyu and Angus steak above the charcoal for your best aftertaste. Every cuisine is an artwork that conceives the pure Japanese taste.
